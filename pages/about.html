DANIEL CACATIAN

Daniel Cacatian is an English and French-speaking 2D/3D artist that operates in Montréal, Canada. He has Bachelor in Fine Arts for Computation Arts from Concordia University, graduated as an honor student with a college degree in Graphic Design from Vanier College. He continues to sharpen his skills as an artist so he can take them into the film and video game industry.
